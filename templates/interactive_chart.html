Explore the data visualization for the Christmas Campaign Budget Optimization project.

About This Visualization

This interactive chart demonstrates the XGBoost model's predictions for marketing channel performance across different budget allocations. Use the interactive controls to explore different scenarios and see how changing allocations affects expected ROI.

The visualization shows both the allocated budget and predicted revenue for each marketing channel based on the model's analysis of historical campaign data. The greatest ROI is shown in the Email channel, followed by SEM, Social Media, and Display advertising.
